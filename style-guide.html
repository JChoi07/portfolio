Colour

Representing an open, energetic and creative nature, orange acts as the primary colour within the portfolio. In support are monchromatic variations of the hue and a double split complimentary counterpart in blue (a complimentary variation is ued in dark mode for greater legibility).

Bright Orange

#FF9425

Used for accenting and highlighting

Light Orange

#FFBB73

Lighter variation used for footer and dark mode

Blue

#0D3FFF

Used for links and underlining text in white spaces for light mode

Light Blue

#26C5FF

Used for links and underlining text in white spaces for dark mode

Dark Mode Black

#373A36

Lighter variation of black used for dark mode

Black

#000000

Used for main text

White

#FFFFFF

Used for the background and text in dark mode

Typography

Inter is the main typeface for this portfolio. Designed for the intention of web use, it’s large x-height provides clarity in communication while still reflecting a fun exterior with it’s rounded dots.
